===========================================
BROWSER EXTENSION ISSUE - HOW TO FIX
===========================================

The 'operata' extension is modifying your links and adding .html to them.

SOLUTION 1: Disable the Extension
----------------------------------
1. Open your browser extensions page:
   - Chrome: chrome://extensions
   - Edge: edge://extensions
   - Firefox: about:addons

2. Find 'operata' or 'pc-override.js' extension

3. Toggle it OFF

4. Refresh http://localhost:3000/

SOLUTION 2: Clear All Browser Data
-----------------------------------
1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete

2. Select:
    Browsing history
    Cookies and other site data
    Cached images and files
   
3. Time range: All time

4. Click 'Clear data'

5. Restart browser

6. Go to http://localhost:3000/

SOLUTION 3: Use Incognito Mode (temporary)
------------------------------------------
Press Ctrl + Shift + N and use http://localhost:3000/

===========================================
YOUR APP IS WORKING PERFECTLY!
The issue is only the browser extension.
===========================================
